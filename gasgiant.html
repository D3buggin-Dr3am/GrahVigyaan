Gas giant
Gas giants are massive planets composed mostly of hydrogen and helium, with no solid surface. Like Jupiter and Saturn, they have thick atmospheres with swirling clouds and intense storms. Some gas giants, called "hot Jupiters," orbit close to their stars, making them extremely hot. Their size and gravity often lead to complex moon systems and rings.
